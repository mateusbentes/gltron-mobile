GLTron Mobile is a 3D light cycle racing game inspired by the classic TRON movie. Race against AI opponents on a digital grid, leaving light trails behind you while avoiding walls and other players' trails.

<b>Features:</b>
• Classic GLTron gameplay with modern mobile controls
• 3D graphics with procedural and FBX model support
• Multiple camera modes (follow and bird's eye view)
• Recognizer (floating probe) with original GLTron movement
• Touch controls optimized for mobile devices
• Keyboard support for desktop/hybrid use
• Sound effects and background music
• Multiple AI difficulty levels
• Score tracking and win/lose detection

<b>Controls:</b>
• Touch: Tap left/right side of screen to turn
• Swipe: Swipe left/right for turning
• Keyboard: Arrow keys or A/D for turning, Space/Enter to start
• Top-left corner: Toggle recognizer on/off
• Top-right corner: Switch camera modes

<b>Gameplay:</b>
Navigate your light cycle around the arena while avoiding walls and light trails. Each player leaves a permanent trail behind them - if you hit a wall or trail, you crash and lose the round. The last player standing wins!

Based on the original GLTron by Andreas Umbach, this mobile adaptation brings the classic light cycle experience to Android devices with modern graphics and intuitive touch controls.

The game is completely free and open source, with no ads or in-app purchases.
