GLTron Mobile (“the App”) is an offline game developed as a fan-made fork of the open-source GLTron project. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

1. No Data Collected

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal data.

2. No Internet Access

The App operates fully offline and does not connect to the Internet or communicate with any external servers.

3. No Permissions Required

The App requires no Android permissions and does not access device features such as storage, camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

4. No Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party SDKs such as analytics, advertisements, crash reporting, or social login.

5. Children's Privacy

Because no data is collected, the App complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, LGPD, and other child data protection laws.

Any future changes will be published on this page.
